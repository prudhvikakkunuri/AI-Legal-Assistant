about

Legal awareness, not legal jargon

Indian law is vast and intimidating — the Constitution, IPC, the newer BNS, CrPC/BNSS, consumer protection, cyber law, and more. Most people don't know their rights simply because the language is inaccessible.

Nyaya AI sits between the user and that complexity. A single, carefully engineered prompt instructs the model to act as an Indian legal advisor, answer in simple English, cite relevant sections or articles where useful, and tell the user clearly whether something is legal or illegal — while always recommending a licensed advocate for serious matters.

what it covers

Built-in legal knowledge areas

The system prompt anchors every answer to specific bodies of Indian law instead of vague generalities.

Constitution & rights

Explains fundamental rights, constitutional articles, and what they actually mean for an ordinary citizen.

"What is Article 21 of the Indian Constitution?"

Criminal law: IPC, BNS, CrPC/BNSS

Covers both the older Indian Penal Code and the newer Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, plus arrest and FIR procedure.

"Can police arrest without a warrant?"

Cyber law & consumer rights

Guides users through cyber fraud, online blackmail, and the consumer complaint process step by step.

"What should I do after a cyber crime?"

Labour, property & family law

Answers everyday questions about employment termination, property disputes, and family legal matters.

"Can my employer fire me without notice?"
capabilities

What it does

Plain-English explanations

Translates dense legal terminology into beginner-friendly language with clear headings and bullet points.

Section & article references

Cites relevant IPC/BNS sections or constitutional articles whenever they're useful to the answer.

Legal vs. illegal verdicts

Directly states whether a described action is legal or illegal, along with likely consequences.

Guided sidebar & examples

A sidebar lists popular query categories and an expander offers ready-made example questions to try.

responsible by design

Guardrails built into the prompt

The system prompt explicitly refuses to assist with illegal activity and always points serious cases toward real legal help.

⚠️ Not a substitute for a licensed advocate. Nyaya AI provides legal information and general awareness only. For serious situations, it explicitly advises consulting a licensed lawyer or approaching the police or legal authorities — and it refuses any request asking how to commit crimes, hack, scam, or evade law enforcement.
